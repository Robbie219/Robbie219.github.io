Robert Harter has been playing the bassoon for 10 years. He is currently completing his Bachelor of Music in Bassoon Performance at the University of Miami Frost School of Music under the tutelage of Gabriel Beavers. Robert has served as Principal, Second, and Contrabassoonist of the Frost Symphony Orchestra under the baton of Thomas Sleeper.

A lover of chamber music, Robert has collaborated extensively with his woodwind trio, Quarter Note = 72, comprised of flutist Mackenzie Miller, clarinetist Kevin Gregory, and himself. Robert also regularly performs recitals featuring both solo and chamber repertoire.

Robert has participated in the Sewanee Summer Music Festival and multiple masterclasses with some of the world's finest bassoonists.

A composer and arranger as well, Robert has had his works performed by many accomplished musicians.
