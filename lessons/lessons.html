Study with me

As a teacher, it is my goal to help students develop the musicianship necessary to becoming sensitive ensemble players and fantastic soloists. I will help you garner the tools you need to become a self-reliant and thoughtful musician. I can get you to the level of playing you need to get into the top music schools and conservatories.

I take students ranging in experience from beginners to advanced high-school level musicians.

I am available for one-on-one lessons, chamber ensemble coachings, reedmaking lessons (both in groups or one-on-one), and masterclasses. We can arrange to have lessons over Skype or in person (depending on your location).
